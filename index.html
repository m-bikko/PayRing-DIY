Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PayRing DIY.

Is PayRing compatible with all payment systems?

PayRing works with all major contactless payment systems including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and mobile payment platforms that support NFC technology. If your card works with tap-to-pay, it will work with PayRing.

How secure is the payment data on the ring?

PayRing uses the same secure encryption technology as your contactless cards. The data transfer process happens locally in your home and never connects to the internet. Additionally, the payment data is linked to your specific card's security features, making unauthorized use extremely difficult.

How do I know which ring size to order?

Our website includes a printable ring sizer guide that you can use to measure your finger accurately. We also offer a sizing kit that can be shipped to you before your main order if you want to be extra certain about the fit.

Do I need any special skills to make the ring?

No special skills are required! Our detailed step-by-step guide makes the process simple for anyone. The most challenging part - creating the ring base - is already done for you with our custom 3D printing. You'll just need to follow the instructions for copying your card data and applying the epoxy resin.

How long does the ring last?

With proper care, your PayRing can last for many years. The epoxy resin creates a durable, waterproof seal that protects the NFC chip. The NFC technology itself has no battery or moving parts, so it won't wear out with normal use.

What if my payment card gets canceled or replaced?

If you get a new payment card, you'll need to create a new PayRing. We offer a discount for returning customers who need to make a replacement ring due to card changes. The NFC chips cannot be reprogrammed once they're sealed in the resin.
